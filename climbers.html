CLIMBERS

BOUGAINVILLEA

The bougainvillea plant is a low-care climber and is
commonly recognised by its clusters of bright hued
flowers. Bougainvillea's flowers create a wonderful
splash of colour.
Botanical Name : Bougainvillea glabra
Family : Nyctaginaceae
Size : 20-30 ft. tall, wide with support

BLUE MORNING GLORY

Award-winning Blue Morning Glory is a vigorous
twining evergreen vine with lush, velvety, heart-shaped
leaves and attractive clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers,
3-4 in. (7-10 cm).
Botanical Name : Ipomoea indica
Family : Convolvulaceae
Size : 4' – 15' (120cm – 4.5m) tall

DEVIL'S IVY

Devil's ivy also called golden pothos, money plant,
hardy indoor foliage plant of the arum family native
to southeastern Asia. It resembles, and thus is often
confused with, the common philodendron.
Botanical Name : Epipremnum aureum
Family : Araceae
Size : 66 ft. tall

STAR JASMINE

Star Jasmine is recognised for its distinct, small,
star-like flowers which feature prominently in the
summer months and boast a pleasant, honeyed
fragrance.
Botanical Name : Trachelospermum jasminoides
Family : Apocynaceae
Size : 60 cm high

BENGAL CLOCK VINE

It is known by many other names such as “Bengal
Trumpet”, “Bengal Clock vine”, “Indian Sky Flower”,
“Sky Vine”, “Blue Trumpet Vine”, “Blue Sky Flower”,
“Blue Thunbergia”; Hindi: Neel latha.
Botanical Name : Thunbergia grandiflora
Family :Acanthaceae
Size : 10-20cm long

SWEET POTATO VINE

Sweet potato vine named for its edible cousin, is
an ornamental warm-season annual grown for its
attractive leaves and vining habit. The foliage lends
a tropical feel to the garden and comes in a range of colors .
Botanical Name : Ipomoea batatas
Family : Convolvulaceae
Size : 2.5 in.
